The Challah Bake

Women from all over greater Chicagoland will come together Wednesday evening, October 21st to make their own challah for the upcoming Shabbat. This essential woman’s mitzvah creates a wonderful atmosphere of camaraderie. The Challah Bake will take place at The Holiday Inn North Shore on Touhy. Seating is limited.

Shabbat

Jews are encouraged to keep a Shabbat in their own communities and synagogues, as well as to create their own “Shabbat Table” of guests. Specific “shul hubs” have been identified to create their own Shabbat meal and programming open to members and guests.
