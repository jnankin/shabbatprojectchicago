How to keep it

Shabbat begins at sunset on Friday and ends at nightfall of the following evening. Here are some tools to help you unlock the magic of keeping Shabbat.

Keep the Shabbat Together

Starts 5:16 p.m., Friday, November 11th
Ends at 6:38 p.m., Saturday, November 12th

More than one million Jews, in 65 cities around the world, are joining together to keep the Shabbos of November 11-12th 2016. It is an act of solidarity, a celebration of our Jewish heritage, and an opportunity to reconnect to our families, friends and ourselves. More than ever, Shabbos is our day of rest. There are many ways to get involved. Join your local synagogue for services, create your own Shabbat Table with your guest list and menu custom-made by you, spend the Shabbat at a host family, challenge yourself to detach from your devices for 25 hours.
